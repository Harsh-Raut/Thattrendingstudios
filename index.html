Questions

Everything you're wondering.

Still unsure? That's normal. Here are the things new singers ask us most.

Is the Voice Test really free?
Yes — completely. No payment, no card, no catch. You book a slot, come in, sing one short piece, and hear yourself on studio monitors. We only ask you to book so every artist gets our full attention.
What if I'm not good enough?
That's exactly why the Voice Test exists. Most singers have never heard themselves recorded properly. You'll be surprised — and if there's something to work on, our engineer will tell you honestly and kindly. There's zero pressure and zero judgement.
Do I need any experience or my own song?
No. Complete first-timers are who we love most. Sing anything you like — a cover, a few lines, a song you wrote. We'll guide the rest.
What happens after the Voice Test?
If you want to make a real, released song, we'll walk you through the packages (from ₹35,000) and build a plan. If you're not ready, that's totally fine — no hard sell, ever. You leave with a clip and a roadmap either way.
Will my song actually get heard?
That's our whole point of difference. Finished songs release on That Trending Song — a channel that already has an audience waiting. Recording is the easy half; getting heard is the part we've already solved.
